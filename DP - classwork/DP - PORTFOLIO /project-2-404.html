Play posters –
404 not found

“404: Not Found” is a promotional poster series created as a design project for a fictional play. The play follows five strangers — known only by tech-inspired aliases like “Bug” and “Cache” — who arrive at a mandatory “digital detox” retreat.
The story quickly unravels into a self-help experiment turning into a psychological thriller as the participants realize they are all connected by a controversial tech company — and that one of them is an imposter.
Aimed at a modern theatre audience interested in psychological thrillers, the series visually promotes themes of surveillance, hidden secrets, fractured identity, and tech-dystopia through dramatic visual design.
The project focuses on capturing escalating psychological tension, developing visual metaphors for digital isolation, and achieving thematic representation tailored for a powerful theatrical marketing campaign.
