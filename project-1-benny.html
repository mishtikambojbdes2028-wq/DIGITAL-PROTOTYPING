Benny the bear

"Benny The Bear Learned to Care" is a children's picture book created as part of a college project for the subject Imaging. The story follows Benny, a young bear who learns the importance of helping around the house. Aimed at early readers, the book promotes values like empathy, responsibility, and teamwork through simple language and engaging illustrations. This project focuses on narrative development, character design, and visual storytelling tailored for a young audience.
